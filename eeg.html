Conversion of EEG SIGNALS to Vowels and Digits

Project information

  • Category: ML
  • Status: Ongoing
  • Project date: August 2022

● A research has been conducted for classifying vowels and digits based on EEG data.
● Primary goals were to generate fresh EEG data for the vowels and to provide a model for classifying EEG signals.
● The study conducted the highest average classification accuracy of 26.2% for Random Forest Algorithm.
